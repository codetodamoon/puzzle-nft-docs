# Connecting Wallet

This guide explains how to connect your Web3 wallet to TreasureHunt.

## Supported Wallets

TreasureHunt supports the following wallets:

- **MetaMask** (Recommended)
- **WalletConnect** (Coming soon)
- **Coinbase Wallet** (Coming soon)

MetaMask is the recommended wallet for the best experience.

## Connecting for the First Time

### Step 1: Click Connect Wallet

Locate the "Connect Wallet" button in the navigation bar and click it.

### Step 2: Select Your Wallet

If using MetaMask, the connection request will appear automatically.

### Step 3: Approve Connection

In your MetaMask wallet:
1. Review the permissions
2. Click "Connect" to approve

### Step 4: Verify Connection

Your wallet address should now be displayed in the navigation bar, truncated (e.g., `0x1234...abcd`).

## Switching Networks

If you're connected to the wrong network:

1. Click your connected address in the navbar
2. A network switch prompt will appear
3. Confirm the switch to the correct network

### Manual Network Switch

If the prompt doesn't appear:

1. Open MetaMask
2. Click the network dropdown
3. Select "BSC Mainnet" or "opBNB Mainnet"

## Disconnecting Your Wallet

To disconnect:

1. Click your address in the navbar
2. Select "Disconnect"
3. Your wallet will be disconnected

## Troubleshooting

### "MetaMask not installed"

**Solution**: Install MetaMask from [metamask.io](https://metamask.io)

### "Connection rejected"

**Solution**:
1. Open MetaMask
2. Go to Settings > Security & Privacy
3. Ensure "Allow connections to dApps" is enabled

### "Wrong network"

**Solution**: Switch to BSC Mainnet or opBNB Mainnet in MetaMask

### "Transaction failed"

**Solution**:
1. Ensure you have enough BNB for gas
2. Check that you're on the correct network
3. Try increasing gas settings in MetaMask

## Security Tips

- Never share your seed phrase
- Always verify contract addresses before interacting
- Check the URL is correct before connecting
- Review transaction details in MetaMask before confirming
